How USDm Powers MegaETH

▶
  • USDm is MegaETH's native stablecoin, backed by Ethena's delta-neutral reserves.
  • Its reserve yield (~3.09% APY) funds sequencer operations across the network.
  • This yield-funded model means gas costs approach zero for end users.
  • Every dollar held in USDm directly subsidizes MegaETH's economic security.
